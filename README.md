# PSL Season 9 — Death Over Batting Analysis

## Question
Which PSL franchise gets the most value from their death over batting (overs 16-20)?

## Data
PSL Season 9 ball-by-ball data from Kaggle — 7,795 individual deliveries 
across all matches.

## Key Findings
1. Multan Sultans had the highest death over batting value at 23.1 runs per 
   wicket, followed by Lahore Qalandars at 21.4. Quetta Gladiators were the 
   weakest at 11.2.

2. Multan Cricket Stadium was the highest scoring death over venue at 77 runs 
   per match, raising the question of whether Multan's dominance was venue 
   driven. However, Multan Sultans performed even better away from home at 
   30.3 runs per wicket vs 23.1 at home — confirming their batting quality 
   was genuine, not just a venue advantage.

3. Lahore Qalandars were the most undisciplined bowling side at 11.2 extras 
   per match. Karachi Kings were the most disciplined at 6.9.

## Tools
Python, pandas, matplotlib
